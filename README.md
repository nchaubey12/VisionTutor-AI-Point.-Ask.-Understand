# 🎓 VisionTutor AI
### *Point your camera. Ask your question. Learn instantly.*

VisionTutor AI turns any webcam into a real-time homework tutor. Point your 
camera at any problem — math, science, grammar — and get instant step-by-step 
explanations, visual diagrams, and practice questions. Live mode enables true 
conversational tutoring: speak naturally, interrupt freely, and get responses 
in under a second.

---

## 🚩 The Problem
Every night, millions of students sit alone with homework they don't understand.
Existing AI tools require typing — slow, frustrating, and disconnected from how 
students actually work. You can't type a complex geometry diagram. You can't 
describe a messy handwritten equation fast enough.

---

## 💡 The Solution
No typing. No uploading. No waiting.
Just point your camera at any homework problem and start talking. The AI sees 
exactly what you see, understands the full context, and responds like a patient 
tutor — in real time.

---

## 🎯 Aim
To make quality tutoring accessible to every student, anywhere, at any time — 
by combining vision AI and conversational audio into a single, frictionless 
learning experience.
